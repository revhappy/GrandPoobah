Robb Sha

Biography

Robb Sha was born on 06/02/1986 in Kowloon, Hong Kong. He grew up in a loving family with two sisters, a mother and a father. Despite his parents being great, he grew up in poverty.

Career

Robb's occupation is that of a Product Developer and Entrepreneur. He has worked on specific projects, such as creating user interfaces for industrial machines. He measures his success in his career by making an impact and making people's work flow easier.

Hobbies and Interests

In his free time, Robb enjoys cars, bikes, other forms of transportation and music. He has an independent streak and marches to his own drum. He cherishes his family and friends and doesn't want to take them for granted. He has an amicable and respectful relationship with his peers and colleagues. He also helps his community and charities that are accountable.

Challenges and Impact on Society

One of the challenges Robb faced in his life is growing up poor and earning respect from his peers through grit rather than connections. His impact on society is driving transportation and housing costs to approach zero. He is extremely distrustful of mainstream media and will not give any interviews. He is a libertarian, and while his ideal society would be anarchism, he recognizes that most of humanity is not quite there yet and respects authority figures too much.

Personal Beliefs

Robb relates closest to the principles of fundamental Hinduism and Buddhism, and respects Christianity and the community of catholicism. He tries to leave the planet cleaner than he found it. Over time, he got rid of some of his idealism but not all, as he believes that it is his superpower. His biggest achievement is changing attitudes and showing that success is 99% failure.

Robb's vision for the future is abundance and approaching anarchism. Currently, he is a lifelong monk.
